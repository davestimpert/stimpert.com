I serve on the Editorial Board for Nature's Scientific Reports and for the Journal of Marine Science and Engineering. I am also an active member of the Acoustical Society of America (Animal Bioacoustics Technical Committee and Women in Acoustics Committee), Society for Marine Mammalogy, and the International Bio-Logging Society (Society Founding Committee, Guest of Executive Committee, Social Outreach Team, Education and Training Working Group, 6th Bio-Logging Symposium (Dependent Care Assistance), 7th Bio-Logging Symposium (Scientific Committee, Dependent Care Assistance, Inclusivity/Diversity)). I focus many of my efforts on career-life balance, developing family-friendly programs, and inclusivity issues within scientific fields.
