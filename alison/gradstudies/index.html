Graduate studies

Thank you for your interest in my Bioacoustics program!  I am a member of the Research Faculty at Moss Landing Marine Labs (MLML/VEL) and can take Master’s students through their Masters of Marine Science graduate program.  I generally look for self-motivated, hard-working, and enthusiastic students who have some sort of experience in the field already (an internship, field experience, data analysis experience, etc.).  MLML is a time-intensive but very rewarding program that will offer you many opportunities, and Monterey Bay is an excellent location for marine science research!  If you are interested in joining my lab, please email me with a bit about yourself, your motivation for wanting to join our program, and *specifically* what research topic(s) you’re interested in, and I will send you some more information about my plans for taking students in the coming year.

Good luck with your search!
